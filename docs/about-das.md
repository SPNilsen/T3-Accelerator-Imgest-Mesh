# About Me

## Douglas Sayles
*Principal Data Scientist, Data and Analytics, Trace3*

With over three decades of experience in the tech industry, I bring a wealth of
knowledge and expertise to my role as a Principal Data Scientist at Trace3. My
career has spanned pivotal roles at Cisco Systems and Hewlett Packard, where I
contributed to advancing computing efficiency and performance. My journey in
data science is fueled by a passion for High-Performance Computing (HPC),
software engineering on Linux, and open-source innovation.

I hold a Master’s Degree in Data Science, specializing in Statistical Learning,
and I thrive on solving complex problems with elegant simplicity. At Trace3, I
lead efforts to orchestrate collaborative computing environments that enable
cutting-edge AI and machine learning solutions, empowering organizations to push
the boundaries of innovation.
