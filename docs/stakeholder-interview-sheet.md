## 3. Interview Objectives
- Understand current usage of Confluence for documentation and knowledge

- Identify challenges in accessing and utilizing existing data for AI

---

## 4. Interview Questions

### Section 1: Current System Usage & Data Management
1. How do you currently use Confluence for documentation and managing knowledge?
2. What types of content are typically stored in Confluence (e.g., project
documentation, meeting notes, knowledge bases)?
3. How do you currently search for and access information within Confluence?

### Section 2: Pain Points & Challenges with Existing Systems
4. What challenges do you face when trying to locate or extract information from
Confluence?
5. Are there specific limitations with the current system that affect your
efficiency in finding relevant content?
6. How would you describe the current process of answering team or client
questions using Confluence?

### Section 3: Potential AI Use Cases & Opportunities
7. How could a Generative AI solution (e.g., chatbot) assist your team in
accessing information more efficiently?
8. What types of questions or requests would you expect an AI chatbot to handle
effectively?
9. Are there specific workflows or tasks where you think an AI-driven assistant
could provide value (e.g., automating responses, summarizing content)?

### Section 4: Analytical & Generative Capabilities
10. What insights or summaries would be useful for an AI solution to generate
from your existing Confluence data?
11. How important is it for the AI to understand context and nuances in
responses (e.g., personalized responses, content-specific recommendations)?
12. Would you be interested in having the AI generate content drafts (e.g.,
reports, summaries, FAQs) based on existing Confluence data?

### Section 5: Desired Features & Improvements
13. What key features would you like to see in an AI-powered chatbot integrated
with Confluence?
14. How important is it for the solution to be scalable and customizable to meet
your team's evolving needs?
15. Are there specific integrations (e.g., Slack, Microsoft Teams) you would
like the AI chatbot to support?

### Section 6: Success Criteria for AI Implementation
16. How would you define success for a Generative AI solution in your
organization?
17. What measurable outcomes (e.g., time saved, user satisfaction, reduced
response times) would indicate a successful implementation?
18. Are there any concerns or risks you foresee with deploying AI-driven
solutions?
