- **High Classification Accuracy**: Achieving precise defect detection across a 
  broad range of defect types.                                                  
- **Operational Throughput**: Meeting production cycle time requirements without
  compromising inspection accuracy.                                                
- **Insights and Recommendations**: Delivering actionable insights into defect  
  classification challenges and potential areas for improvement.

    - Performance metrics for classification accuracy under production conditions.
